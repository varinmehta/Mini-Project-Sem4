About

Welcome to Tweeting for Protection, an innovative project dedicated to the conservation of endangered species. We utilize advanced voice recognition technology to identify and monitor these species through their distinctive audio signatures.

Through user-contributed audio recordings, we analyze and match patterns, aiding in the identification of endangered species. This information is invaluable for researchers and conservationists working towards preserving biodiversity and ecosystems.

Join us in this mission by uploading audio recordings of bird calls. Together, we can make a significant impact on wildlife preservation and create a harmonious balance in our natural world.

Audio

Upload an audio recording of a bird call, and our system will analyze it to determine if it matches the patterns of endangered species. Your contribution helps in monitoring and protecting wildlife.
