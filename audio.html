Audio Analysis

Help protect endangered species by contributing audio recordings of bird calls. Our advanced system analyzes the patterns and aids in monitoring and protecting wildlife.

Tip: Ensure your audio file is in a standard format (e.g., MP3, WAV) for accurate analysis.

Why Contribute?

Your audio recordings can help us identify the species of the bird, which will inturn help us conserve them and track them if they are endangered.
